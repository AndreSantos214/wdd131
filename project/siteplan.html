Enchanted Sweets - Site Plan

Site Name

Enchanted Sweets

This name represents a magical and delightful experience with our cake-in-a-jar desserts. The word "Enchanted" evokes a sense of wonder and special moments, while "Sweets" clearly indicates our delicious dessert offerings. This name perfectly captures the magical experience of enjoying our unique cake-in-a-jar creations.

Domain: enchanted-sweets.com

Site Purpose

The Enchanted Sweets website serves as a digital storefront for our small business specializing in cake-in-a-jar desserts. The site provides customers with information about our unique dessert offerings, our story as a couple pursuing our wedding dreams, and a platform to showcase our delicious cake-in-a-jar flavors. The website aims to attract customers, share our passion for baking, and help us raise funds for our upcoming wedding while building a sustainable dessert business.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: What cake-in-a-jar flavors do you offer and what are the ingredients in each one?
  • Scenario 2: How can I place an order for cake-in-a-jar desserts for my special event?
  • Scenario 3: What's the story behind Enchanted Sweets and why did you start this business?
